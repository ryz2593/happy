Create this look in real life

Eyes
Eyeshadow:
Tip: For this look, we blended colors to get the right shades. Go for a soft purple-pink on the lid. Work a darker purple into the crease and outer corners (but don't overdo it), and use a light lilac above the crease.
Top eyeliner: Pinup.
Bottom eyeliner: One-third of lash line, starting at outer lash line.
Liner color: Black.
Top lashes: Doll - voluptuous and thick. Use a lash primer for that added volume!
Bottom lashes: Middle lash line to outer lash line, full volume.
Lash color: Black.
Brows: Natural. For lightweight hold, keep brows in place with a colorless gel or clear chubby wax pencil. For soft definition and color, use a spoolie brush to apply a slightly tinted brow gel. Brush up and away from the center of your face.

Lipstick:
Gloss: Try a sheer gloss or shimmery pink.
